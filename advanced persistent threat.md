- APT 1

- Salt Typhoon
  - Salt Typhoon is a Chinese state-sponsored cyber espionage group believed to be operated by China's Ministry of State Security (MSS). Active since at least 2020, the group has conducted high-profile cyber espionage campaigns, particularly against the United States. Their operations place an emphasis on counterintelligence targets in the U.S. and data theft of key corporate intellectual property. The group has infiltrated targets in dozens of other countries on nearly every continent.

- Volt Typhoon
  - Volt Typhoon is a Chinese state-sponsored cyber espionage group active since at least mid-2021, primarily targeting critical infrastructure in the United States, including sectors such as energy, telecommunications, transportation, and water systems. Known for their stealthy "living off the land" tactics, they use legitimate administrative tools to infiltrate networks and avoid detection. Their operations focus on long-term surveillance and intelligence gathering, potentially to prepare for disruptive actions during geopolitical conflicts, especially concerning Taiwan.
