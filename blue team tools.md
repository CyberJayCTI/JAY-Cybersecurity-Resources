### Blue Team Tools

#### Digital Forensics Tools

- [Autopsy](https://www.autopsy.com) - Digital forensics tool that helps investigators analyze hard drives, smartphones, and other storage devices to recover and examine evidence such as deleted files, internet history, emails, and metadata.

- [FTK Imager](https://www.exterro.com/digital-forensics-software/ftk-imager) -  Forensic tool used to create exact copies (forensic images) of data from storage devices and preview their contents without altering the original evidence.
